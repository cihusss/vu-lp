Earn Agile & Scrum Certification

Successfully complete three required courses and a final project to earn your Certificate in Agile. Students can then choose to take a certification exam to earn Villanova's Professional Certification in Agile and Scrum credential. Earning this certification can help demonstrate your commitment to learning and applying Agile and Scrum best practices.

Prepare for Agile Certification Exams

The Certificate in Agile curriculum aligns with multiple certifying bodies, allowing students to prepare to sit for certification exams from the following industry-recognized certifying bodies:

  • Project Management Institute (PMI): PMI-Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP®)

PMI-Agile Certified Practitioner and PMI-ACP are registered marks of the Project Management Institute, Inc.

  • Tips for passing industry-recognized Agile certification exams
